Who are we?

At Goals! 5-a-side football we believe that fitness should be fun and accessible to everyone young and old. Thats why we have 3 state of the art artificial pitches that have floodlights so you can play football any time in any weather. We have football leagues so you can join as a team or you can book a pitch for upto two hours. If you just want to come along and show your support we have some light refreshments available as well.
